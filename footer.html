FAQ

What is a topi ?
Topi in India and many South Asian countries means a hat in all its variations. For sake of completeness, Webster defines topi as a sub-Saharan antelope also - that is just coincidental, not our focus at all.
What are the execution plans ?
Initially, we will feature hats from India only. Within a year, we will diversify to other regions beginning with Africa and South America. We have already identified a source in India.
