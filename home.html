Admittedly, this is a very simple idea. It clearly falls in the category of a small business and not a startup. Moreover, before delving into a complex web application, this project seemed manageable. As much as we would like to see this effort succeed, at the very least, we will gain an understanding of the key technologies involved in the deployment of a modern ecommerce website. In addition, it will be interesting to see how this website can be promoted on the internet using Google or Facebook market analytics tools.

Motivation:

Promotion of indigenous crafts: This is one of the key objectives of the enterprise. Beginning with hats from Himanchal Pradesh, a mountainous state in India, we will soon cover rest of India. Within a year, we will start showcasing hats from Africa and South America. Gradually, we plan to cover rest of the world.

Fair Trade: One of the bedrock principles of the business is a firm commitment to fair trade. We will keep interest and well being of the communities we interact with front and center.

Evolution: Although we will be offering only generic designs initially, there are plans to offer customized hats in not too distant future. We envision this site evolving as a market place for ethnic hats providing a social platform for artisans and consumers. We aspire to be the 'portal for all topi aficionados'.
